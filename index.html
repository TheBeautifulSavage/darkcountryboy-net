About Dark Country Boy

Dark Country Boy is an independent artist carving a singular niche at the crossroads of dark country, Americana, and Southern Gothic storytelling. With 70 albums and over 1,400 tracks released since 2025, the music draws from the deep wells of Appalachian tradition, outlaw country, and blues — wrapped in modern production that never loses its grit. Ghost stories set to steel guitar. War hymns on open tuning. The American experience, unfiltered.
